# Approver Guide

## Overview

As an approver, you play a critical role in the concept paper approval workflow. This guide covers the responsibilities and procedures for all approver roles: SPS (School Principal/Supervisor), VP Academic Affairs, Auditor, and Accounting.

## Approver Roles

### SPS (School Principal/Supervisor)

-   **Stage**: 1 - SPS Review
-   **Time Limit**: 1 business day
-   **Responsibilities**: Initial review and approval of concept papers
-   **Focus**: Completeness, appropriateness, and alignment with department goals

### VP Academic Affairs

-   **Stages**: 2 - VP Acad Review, 4 - Acad Copy Distribution
-   **Time Limits**: 3 days (review), 1 day (distribution)
-   **Responsibilities**: Academic review, approval, and distribution of academic copies
-   **Focus**: Academic merit, policy compliance, and proper documentation

### Auditor

-   **Stages**: 3 - Auditing Review, 5 - Auditing Copy Distribution, 7 - Audit & Countersign
-   **Time Limits**: 3 days (review), 1 day (distribution), 1 day (countersign)
-   **Responsibilities**: Audit review, distribution, and countersigning of vouchers
-   **Focus**: Financial compliance, policy adherence, and proper documentation

### Accounting

-   **Stages**: 6 - Voucher Preparation, 8 - Cheque Preparation, 9 - Budget Release
-   **Time Limits**: 1 day (voucher), 4 days (cheque), 1 day (release)
-   **Responsibilities**: Financial processing, voucher and cheque preparation, budget release
-   **Focus**: Accurate financial processing and timely payment

## Reviewing Concept Papers

### Accessing Pending Approvals

#### From Dashboard

1. Log in to the system
2. Your dashboard shows:
    - Number of pending tasks
    - Overdue items (highlighted in red)
    - Recently completed tasks
3. Click **"View Pending Approvals"**

#### From Navigation Menu

1. Click **"Pending Approvals"** in the main menu
2. You'll see all papers assigned to you
3. Papers are sorted by due date (oldest first)

### Understanding the Pending List

Each paper shows:

-   **Tracking Number** - Unique identifier
-   **Title** - Concept paper title
-   **Requisitioner** - Who submitted it
-   **Department** - Submitting department
-   **Stage** - Current workflow stage
-   **Due Date** - Deadline for completion
-   **Status** - Pending or Overdue
-   **Actions** - Review button

### Reviewing a Paper

#### Step 1: Open Paper Details

1. Click on a paper or click **"Review"**
2. The paper details page opens

#### Step 2: Review Information

Examine all sections:

**Header Information:**

-   Tracking number and title
-   Submission date and requisitioner
-   Current stage and due date

**Paper Details:**

-   Nature of request (Regular, Urgent, Emergency)
-   Department information
-   Complete concept paper details

**Workflow Timeline:**

-   Visual representation of all stages
-   Completed stages (green)
-   Current stage (highlighted)
-   Your stage in the workflow

**Audit Trail:**

-   Complete history of actions
-   Previous approver remarks
-   Stage transitions
-   Timestamps

**Attachments:**

-   Original concept paper (PDF)
-   Supporting documents
-   Download and review all files

#### Step 3: Review the Document

1. Download the concept paper PDF
2. Review thoroughly:
    - Check completeness
    - Verify budget calculations
    - Assess appropriateness
    - Check policy compliance
    - Review supporting documentation

#### Step 4: Make a Decision

You have three options:

1. **Complete Stage** - Approve and advance to next stage
2. **Return Paper** - Send back for revision
3. **Add Attachment** - Upload supporting documents (optional)

## Completing Workflow Stages

### When to Complete a Stage

Complete a stage when:

-   You've thoroughly reviewed the paper
-   All requirements are met
-   Information is complete and accurate
-   Budget is appropriate
-   Policy compliance is verified
-   No issues or concerns exist

### How to Complete a Stage

#### Step 1: Review Completion Requirements

Ensure you've:

-   Reviewed all documents
-   Verified all information
-   Checked for completeness
-   Assessed appropriateness
-   Confirmed policy compliance

#### Step 2: Add Remarks (Optional)

1. Scroll to the **"Complete Stage"** section
2. Enter remarks in the text field (optional but recommended)
3. Provide context or notes for next approver
4. Highlight any important points

**Example Remarks:**

-   "Approved. Budget is reasonable and well-justified."
-   "All documentation is complete. Proceeding to next stage."
-   "Reviewed and approved. Note: Urgent request, expedite processing."

#### Step 3: Complete the Stage

1. Click **"Complete Stage"** button
2. Confirm your action
3. The paper advances to the next stage
4. Next approver is notified automatically
5. You receive confirmation

### After Completion

Once completed:

-   Paper moves to next stage
-   Next approver is assigned
-   Requisitioner is notified
-   Your task is removed from pending list
-   Action is recorded in audit trail

### Stage-Specific Completion

#### SPS Review (Stage 1)

-   Verify requisitioner information
-   Check department alignment
-   Assess initial appropriateness
-   Approve to advance to VP Acad

#### VP Acad Review (Stage 2)

-   Conduct academic review
-   Verify policy compliance
-   Check budget justification
-   Approve to advance to Auditing

#### Auditing Review (Stage 3)

-   Perform audit review
-   Check financial compliance
-   Verify documentation
-   Approve to advance to distribution

#### Acad Copy Distribution (Stage 4)

-   Confirm academic copy distributed
-   Verify receipt
-   Complete to advance

#### Auditing Copy Distribution (Stage 5)

-   Confirm auditing copy distributed
-   Verify receipt
-   Complete to advance

#### Voucher Preparation (Stage 6)

-   Prepare payment voucher
-   Attach voucher document
-   Verify accuracy
-   Complete to advance

#### Audit & Countersign (Stage 7)

-   Review voucher
-   Verify accuracy
-   Countersign voucher
-   Complete to advance

#### Cheque Preparation (Stage 8)

-   Prepare payment cheque
-   Process payment
-   Attach documentation
-   Complete to advance

#### Budget Release (Stage 9)

-   Finalize payment
-   Release budget
-   Complete process
-   Mark as completed

## Returning Papers with Remarks

### When to Return a Paper

Return a paper when:

-   Information is incomplete
-   Documentation is missing
-   Budget is unclear or unjustified
-   Policy violations exist
-   Clarification is needed
-   Errors are present

### How to Return a Paper

#### Step 1: Identify Issues

Document specific problems:

-   What information is missing?
-   What needs clarification?
-   What errors exist?
-   What policy concerns are there?

#### Step 2: Write Clear Remarks

1. Scroll to the **"Return Paper"** section
2. Enter detailed remarks (required)
3. Be specific and constructive
4. List all issues clearly
5. Provide guidance for correction

**Good Remarks Examples:**

"Returning for the following reasons:

1. Budget breakdown is missing detailed line items
2. Justification for equipment cost needs more detail
3. Please include quotes from at least two vendors
4. Resubmit with complete budget documentation"

"Paper returned for clarification:

-   The project timeline is unclear
-   Budget allocation needs breakdown by category
-   Please specify the department beneficiaries
-   Include supporting documentation for major expenses"

**Poor Remarks Examples:**

-   "Incomplete" (too vague)
-   "Fix budget" (not specific)
-   "Missing info" (doesn't say what)

#### Step 3: Return the Paper

1. Click **"Return Paper"** button
2. Confirm your action
3. Paper is sent back to requisitioner
4. Requisitioner is notified with your remarks
5. Status changes to "Returned"

### After Returning

Once returned:

-   Requisitioner receives notification
-   Your remarks are visible in audit trail
-   Paper is removed from your pending list
-   Requisitioner must create new submission
-   New submission will come back through workflow

### Best Practices for Returns

**Be Specific:**

-   List each issue clearly
-   Provide examples when helpful
-   Reference specific sections or pages

**Be Constructive:**

-   Explain what's needed
-   Provide guidance for correction
-   Suggest resources if applicable

**Be Professional:**

-   Use respectful language
-   Focus on the paper, not the person
-   Maintain a helpful tone

**Be Complete:**

-   Address all issues at once
-   Don't return multiple times for different issues
-   Provide comprehensive feedback

## Adding Attachments and Supporting Documents

### When to Add Attachments

Add attachments when:

-   Supporting documentation is required
-   Approval forms need to be attached
-   Additional information is helpful
-   Vouchers or receipts are needed
-   Official documents must be included

### Types of Attachments

**Common Attachments by Role:**

**SPS:**

-   Approval forms
-   Department endorsements

**VP Academic Affairs:**

-   Academic approval forms
-   Policy compliance documents
-   Distribution receipts

**Auditor:**

-   Audit review forms
-   Compliance documents
-   Countersigned vouchers

**Accounting:**

-   Payment vouchers
-   Cheques
-   Financial documents
-   Receipts

### How to Add Attachments

#### Step 1: Prepare Your Document

1. Ensure document is in PDF format
2. Check file size (max 10MB)
3. Use descriptive filename
4. Verify document is complete

#### Step 2: Upload Attachment

1. Scroll to **"Add Attachment"** section
2. Click **"Choose File"** or drag and drop
3. Select your PDF file
4. Add optional description
5. Click **"Upload"**

#### Step 3: Verify Upload

1. Attachment appears in attachments list
2. Verify correct file was uploaded
3. Check that it's accessible
4. Continue with stage completion or return

### Managing Attachments

**Viewing Attachments:**

-   All attachments are visible on paper details page
-   Shows who uploaded and when
-   Download any attachment to review

**Attachment Information:**

-   Filename
-   Upload date and time
-   Uploaded by (user name)
-   File size
-   Description (if provided)

## Managing Deadlines and Overdue Notifications

### Understanding Deadlines

Each stage has a maximum processing time:

-   **1 Day**: SPS Review, Acad Distribution, Auditing Distribution, Voucher Prep, Audit & Countersign, Budget Release
-   **3 Days**: VP Acad Review, Auditing Review
-   **4 Days**: Cheque Preparation

### Deadline Calculation

Deadlines are calculated in business days:

-   Excludes weekends
-   Excludes holidays
-   Starts when stage is assigned
-   Ends at end of business day

### Overdue Indicators

When a stage becomes overdue:

**Visual Indicators:**

-   Red badge on paper
-   Red highlight in pending list
-   Overdue count on dashboard

**Notifications:**

-   Email notification to you
-   Notification to administrator
-   Daily reminders until completed

### Managing Your Workload

**Prioritize Tasks:**

1. Overdue items first (red)
2. Items due today
3. Items due soon
4. Regular items

**Time Management:**

-   Review pending list daily
-   Complete simple tasks quickly
-   Schedule time for complex reviews
-   Don't let items become overdue

**When You're Unavailable:**

-   Notify administrator in advance
-   Request temporary reassignment
-   Complete pending tasks before leave
-   Provide coverage arrangements

### Handling Overdue Papers

If you have overdue papers:

1. **Review immediately** - Prioritize overdue items
2. **Complete or return** - Take action quickly
3. **Communicate** - Inform administrator if issues exist
4. **Prevent recurrence** - Improve time management

## Role-Specific Workflows

### SPS Workflow

**Your Responsibilities:**

-   First point of review
-   Verify basic completeness
-   Assess departmental appropriateness
-   Quick turnaround (1 day)

**Review Checklist:**

-   [ ] Requisitioner information complete
-   [ ] Department is correct
-   [ ] Title is clear and descriptive
-   [ ] PDF document is attached and readable
-   [ ] Request seems appropriate
-   [ ] No obvious issues or concerns

**Common Actions:**

-   Approve most papers quickly
-   Return if obviously incomplete
-   Add remarks for next approver if needed

### VP Academic Affairs Workflow

**Your Responsibilities:**

-   Academic review and approval
-   Policy compliance verification
-   Distribution of academic copies
-   Two stages (Review and Distribution)

**Review Checklist:**

-   [ ] Academic merit is clear
-   [ ] Aligns with institutional goals
-   [ ] Budget is reasonable
-   [ ] Policy compliance verified
-   [ ] Documentation is complete
-   [ ] Justification is adequate

**Distribution Checklist:**

-   [ ] Academic copy prepared
-   [ ] Copy distributed to appropriate parties
-   [ ] Receipt confirmed
-   [ ] Documentation attached

### Auditor Workflow

**Your Responsibilities:**

-   Audit review and compliance
-   Distribution of auditing copies
-   Voucher countersigning
-   Three stages throughout process

**Review Checklist:**

-   [ ] Financial compliance verified
-   [ ] Budget is appropriate
-   [ ] Documentation is complete
-   [ ] Policy adherence confirmed
-   [ ] No red flags or concerns

**Countersign Checklist:**

-   [ ] Voucher is accurate
-   [ ] Amounts are correct
-   [ ] Documentation supports payment
-   [ ] Ready for payment processing

### Accounting Workflow

**Your Responsibilities:**

-   Financial processing
-   Voucher preparation
-   Cheque preparation
-   Budget release
-   Three stages at end of process

**Voucher Preparation:**

-   [ ] Create payment voucher
-   [ ] Verify budget allocation
-   [ ] Attach voucher document
-   [ ] Ensure accuracy

**Cheque Preparation:**

-   [ ] Process payment
-   [ ] Prepare cheque
-   [ ] Verify amounts
-   [ ] Attach documentation

**Budget Release:**

-   [ ] Finalize payment
-   [ ] Release budget
-   [ ] Complete all documentation
-   [ ] Mark process complete

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Complete Stage

**Problem**: Complete button is disabled

**Solutions**:

-   Verify you're assigned to this stage
-   Check that stage is current
-   Ensure paper isn't already completed
-   Refresh the page
-   Contact administrator

### Cannot Upload Attachment

**Problem**: File won't upload

**Solutions**:

-   Check file format (must be PDF)
-   Verify file size (under 10MB)
-   Try different browser
-   Check internet connection
-   Compress PDF if too large

### Missing Papers

**Problem**: Expected paper not in pending list

**Solutions**:

-   Check "All Papers" view
-   Verify it's assigned to you
-   Check if already completed
-   Look in completed list
-   Contact administrator

### Notification Issues

**Problem**: Not receiving notifications

**Solutions**:

-   Check email spam folder
-   Verify email in profile
-   Check notification settings
-   Contact administrator
-   Use in-app notifications
