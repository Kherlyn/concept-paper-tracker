# Requisitioner Guide

## Overview

As a requisitioner, you are responsible for submitting concept papers for budget allocation and tracking them through the approval process. This guide will help you understand how to create submissions, monitor their progress, and manage your papers effectively.

## Submitting Concept Papers

### Before You Begin

Prepare the following information:

-   **Concept Paper Title** - A clear, descriptive title
-   **Department** - Your department name
-   **Nature of Request** - Regular, Urgent, or Emergency
-   **PDF Document** - Your concept paper in PDF format (max 10MB)

### Step-by-Step Submission Process

#### Step 1: Access the Submission Form

1. Log in to the system
2. Click **"Submit Paper"** in the main navigation
3. You will see the concept paper submission form

#### Step 2: Fill in Required Information

Complete all required fields:

**Requisitioner Name**

-   Your full name (automatically filled from your profile)
-   Verify this is correct

**Department**

-   Your department or unit
-   This helps route the paper appropriately

**Concept Paper Title**

-   Provide a clear, descriptive title
-   Be specific about the purpose or project
-   Example: "Research Equipment Purchase for Computer Science Lab"

**Nature of Request**

-   **Regular** - Standard processing timeline (most common)
-   **Urgent** - Requires faster processing (provide justification)
-   **Emergency** - Critical need (requires special approval)

#### Step 3: Upload Your Document

1. Click **"Choose File"** or drag and drop your PDF
2. Ensure your file meets requirements:
    - **Format**: PDF only
    - **Size**: Under 10MB
    - **Quality**: Clear and readable
    - **Content**: Complete concept paper with all required sections

**Document Best Practices:**

-   Use a descriptive filename
-   Ensure all pages are included
-   Check that text is readable
-   Include all necessary signatures (if required)
-   Verify budget details are accurate

#### Step 4: Review Your Information

Before submitting:

1. Review all fields for accuracy
2. Verify the uploaded document is correct
3. Check that all required information is provided
4. Ensure contact information is current

#### Step 5: Submit

1. Click **"Submit Concept Paper"**
2. You will receive a confirmation message
3. A unique tracking number will be assigned
4. The first approver (SPS) will be notified automatically

### After Submission

Once submitted:

-   You'll receive a confirmation email
-   The paper enters Stage 1 (SPS Review)
-   You can track progress in "My Papers"
-   You'll receive notifications at each stage
-   The tracking number can be used for reference

## Tracking Submissions

### Viewing Your Papers

#### Accessing Your Papers List

1. Navigate to **"My Papers"** in the main menu
2. You will see a table of all your submissions

#### Understanding the Papers List

Each paper displays:

-   **Tracking Number** - Unique identifier (e.g., CP-2024-001)
-   **Title** - Your concept paper title
-   **Current Stage** - Where the paper is in the workflow
-   **Status** - Current state (Pending, In Progress, Completed, Returned)
-   **Submission Date** - When you submitted the paper
-   **Actions** - View details button

#### Status Indicators

**Pending (Yellow Badge)**

-   Paper is waiting for action
-   Assigned to an approver
-   No action required from you

**In Progress (Yellow Badge)**

-   Paper is being actively reviewed
-   Approver is working on it
-   Monitor for updates

**Completed (Green Badge)**

-   Paper has successfully completed all stages
-   Budget has been released
-   Process is finished

**Returned (Red Badge)**

-   Paper has been sent back for revision
-   Action required from you
-   Check remarks for details

**Overdue (Red Badge)**

-   A stage has exceeded its time limit
-   May require follow-up
-   Contact administrator if needed

### Viewing Paper Details

#### Accessing Details

1. Click on any paper in your list
2. Or click the **"View"** button

#### Paper Details Page

The details page shows:

**Header Information:**

-   Tracking number
-   Title
-   Current status
-   Submission date

**Requisitioner Information:**

-   Your name
-   Department
-   Contact information

**Paper Details:**

-   Nature of request
-   Current stage
-   Assigned approver
-   Due date for current stage

**Visual Workflow Timeline:**

-   All 9 stages displayed
-   Completed stages (green checkmark)
-   Current stage (highlighted)
-   Pending stages (gray)
-   Overdue stages (red)

**Audit Trail:**

-   Complete history of all actions
-   Who did what and when
-   Remarks from approvers
-   Stage transitions

**Attachments:**

-   Your original submission
-   Supporting documents added by approvers
-   Download any attachment

### Monitoring Progress

#### Check Your Dashboard

Your dashboard provides quick overview:

-   Total papers submitted
-   Papers by status
-   Recent activity
-   Quick actions

#### Enable Notifications

Stay informed with notifications:

-   Email notifications (automatic)
-   In-app notifications (bell icon)

-   Return notifications
-   Overdue warnings

#### Understanding Timeline

Typical processing time:

-   **Minimum**: 16 business days

-   **Varies based on**: Returns, urgency, approver availability

## Managing Attachments

### Viewing Attachments

#### On Paper Details Page

1. Scroll to the **"Attachments"** section
2. You'll see all files associated with the paper
3. Each attachment shows:
    - Filename
    - Type (Initial Submission, Supporting Document)
    - Uploaded by
    - Upload date
    - File size

#### Downloading Attachments

1. Click the **"Download"** button next to any attachment
2. File will download to your computer
3. Open with appropriate PDF reader

### Understanding Attachment Types

**Initial Submission**

-   Your original uploaded concept paper
-   Cannot be modified after submission
-   Permanent record of your request

**Supporting Documents**

-   Added by approvers during review
-   May include:
    -   Additional requirements
    -   Approval forms
    -   Budget justifications
    -   Vouchers
    -   Receipts

### When Approvers Add Attachments

Approvers may add documents:

-   To provide additional information
-   To attach required forms
-   To document approvals
-   To include financial documents

You'll receive a notification when new attachments are added.

## Handling Returned Papers

### When a Paper is Returned

If an approver returns your paper:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Status changes to "Returned" (red badge)
3. Paper appears at top of your list
4. Action is required from you

### Understanding Return Reasons

Check the remarks to understand why:

-   Missing information
-   Incomplete documentation
-   Budget concerns
-   Policy issues
-   Clarification needed

### Reviewing Return Remarks

1. Open the paper details
2. Check the **Audit Trail** section
3. Find the return action
4. Read the remarks carefully
5. Note what needs to be corrected

### Addressing Issues

To resolve the return:

1. **Review the remarks** - Understand what's needed
2. **Gather information** - Collect missing details or documents
3. **Prepare corrections** - Update your concept paper if needed
4. **Contact approver** - If clarification is needed
5. **Resubmit** - Create a new submission with corrections

### Resubmitting

**Important**: You cannot edit a returned paper. You must:

1. Create a new submission
2. Include all corrected information
3. Upload the revised document
4. Reference the original tracking number in the title or document
5. Submit as a new paper

**Example Title**: "Research Equipment Purchase (Revised - CP-2024-001)"

### Preventing Returns

To minimize returns:

-   Provide complete information initially
-   Follow all guidelines and policies
-   Include all required documentation
-   Verify budget calculations
-   Review before submitting
-   Consult with approvers if unsure

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Standard Submission

**Situation**: Regular concept paper for department supplies

**Steps**:

1. Prepare complete concept paper with budget
2. Submit through the system
3. Monitor progress through 9 stages
4. Receive completion notification
5. Budget released in 16-25 days

### Scenario 2: Urgent Request

**Situation**: Time-sensitive equipment purchase

**Steps**:

1. Select "Urgent" as nature of request
2. Include justification in document
3. Notify relevant approvers
4. Monitor closely for any issues
5. Expect faster processing

**Situation**: SPS returns paper for missing budget details

1. Receive return notification
2. Review remarks in audit trail
3. Update concept paper with budget details
4. Create new submission with corrections
5. Reference original tracking number
6. Resubmit

### Scenario 4: Overdue Stage

**Situation**: Paper stuck at VP Acad stage for 5 days

**Steps**:

1. Note overdue indicator (red)
2. Check if approver is available
3. Contact administrator if needed
4. Wait for resolution
5. Monitor for progress

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Submit Paper

**Problem**: Submit button is disabled

**Solutions**:

-   Verify all required fields are filled
-   Check that PDF is uploaded
-   Ensure file size is under 10MB
-   Try refreshing the page
-   Clear browser cache

### File Upload Fails

**Problem**: PDF won't upload

**Solutions**:

-   Check file size (must be under 10MB)
-   Verify file format (must be PDF)
-   Try a different browser
-   Check internet connection
-   Compress PDF if too large

### Cannot View Paper Details

**Problem**: Details page won't load

**Solutions**:

-   Refresh the page
-   Check internet connection
-   Try different browser
-   Clear browser cache
-   Contact support if persists

### Missing Notifications

**Problem**: Not receiving email notifications

**Solutions**:

-   Check spam/junk folder
-   Verify email address in profile
-   Check notification settings
-   Contact administrator
-   Use in-app notifications
